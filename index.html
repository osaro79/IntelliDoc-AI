Frequently Asked Questions

Are the documents legally binding?

Yes, all documents generated by IntelliDoc AI are legally binding and comply with relevant state and local laws based on your jurisdiction selection.

How quickly will I receive my document?

Standard delivery is within 60 minutes. Premium subscribers receive their documents within 30 minutes.

Who reviews the documents?

All documents are reviewed by licensed paralegals or attorneys specialized in real estate law before delivery.

Can I edit the documents after receiving them?

Yes, documents are delivered in both PDF and editable formats, allowing you to make changes if needed.

What types of documents can I generate?

We offer a wide range of legal documents including eviction notices, lease agreements, property damage notices, rent increase notifications, and more.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption to protect your data. We never share your information with third parties.
